Best Horror Movies of All Time

  1. Nightmare on Elm Street- The ghost of a child murderer, who was killed by a mob of vigalantees begins invading the dreams of teenagers.
  2. The Conjuring- In 1970, 2 demonologists investigate an old farmhouse with an awful history.
  3. Scream- Director Wes Craven exposes all of the most famous horror movie cliques in this meta slasher film where a masked killer stalks the adolesent population of a small town.
  4. The Cabin in the Woods- A group of college students are killed off, one by one, in a ritual to appease "The Ancient Ones."
  5. Poltergeist- A suburban family moves into a newly constructed house, but soon begins to experiance supernatural occurances. Their young daughter is eventually kidnapped by the spirits.
