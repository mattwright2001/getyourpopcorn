Best Action Movies of All Time

  1. Raiders of the Lost Ark- Archeologist Indiana Jones must find the lost Ark of the Covenant before the Nazis do.
  2. Terminator- After Skynet, a robotics manufactoror, becomes sentient, they go to war against humans. At the begining of this film humans have all but won, but Skynet has one secret weapon in its sleeve, the Terminator. The terminator is sent back in time to the 80s to kill Sarah Conner, mother of the human's general John Conner before he can be born.
  3. Inglorious Bastards- The stories of Nazi leader Hans "Jew Hunter" Landa, Lt. Aldo Raine and his team of Jewish soldiers, and Shosanna Dreyfus are interwoven in this WWII story.
  4. 300- King Leonidas and his army of 300 Spartans fight for this land in a suicide mission at Thermopylae
